  • Wild Africa

    • You don't have to get out of Singapore to experience the splendour of the African plains

    - Lions: Come visit Simba, the first lion cub conceived through assisted reproduction in Singapore, and his family!
    - Cheetahs: With slender, long-legged bodies capable of up to 115km/h in top speeds, try and catch a glimpse of the fastest land mammal!
    - Giraffes: Meet the four giants - Adhil, Balaji, Jubilee and Marco - as they display their natural behaviour of feeding off the highest leaves in the trees.
